MEANING OF CSUC LOGO

The meaning of the Christian Service University College is
derived from the constituent elements. The logo has the following elements:

Statement of Motto: "To Know Christ Better and to Make Him Better Known"

Year of Establishment of the Institution: 1974

Symbols of the Institution: "The Cross of Christ";'Atumpan' - The "talking drums" of the Akans.

TheCross of Christ represents the gospel message of salavation.

The Atumpan was used by the Akans in the olden days to send
messages to people within the community. In the logo, the Atumpan drums represents the primary purpose of the University College. To make Christ better known.
